Chaos – Unpredictability, Survival

Chaos trials throw ever-changing rules and random challenges, testing adaptability and survival instincts.

"Randomizer"

Type: Solo / Teams

Every 30 seconds, a rule changes:

  • Inverted controls
  • Gravity swap
  • No jumping
  • All players visible on radar

Survive 3 rounds of chaos.

"Snake Eyes"

Type: Solo Player

Roll a dice. Complete a challenge based on the number:

  • 1: Solve a puzzle blind
  • 6: Outrun a stampede
  • Randomized tasks tied to dice outcomes

"Spin Room"

Type: Teams

Circular spinning platform with platforms, spikes, and projectiles.

Direction changes every 10 seconds.
