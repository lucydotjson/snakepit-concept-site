Social – Teamwork, Influence

"Linked Fates"

Duo challenge

Players are tethered in pairs and must complete a cooperative obstacle course.

When they reach the end, they must vote — only one can move on.

"Vote to Save"

Group strategy

Four groups solve puzzles in parallel.

Afterward, all players vote to save one group. The least voted group is eliminated.

"Fame or Shame"

Individual influence

Players rate each other after completing a task.

The most and least voted players move forward. Everyone else is eliminated.
