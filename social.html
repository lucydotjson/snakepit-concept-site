Social – Teamwork, Influence

Social trials demand communication, cooperation, and strategy — but only one moves forward.

"Linked Fates"

Players are tethered in pairs.

Complete a platforming course together.

At the end, only one can move on — mutual vote or backstab.

"Vote to Save"

Four groups compete in parallel puzzles.

Players vote to save one other group. Lowest voted group is eliminated.

"Fame or Shame"

Players vote on MVPs of a task.

Highest and lowest fame both get to the next round. Middle? Eliminated.
