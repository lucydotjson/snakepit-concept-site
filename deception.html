Deception – Lies, Sabotage

Deception trials put your trust and deduction skills to the test. Who’s lying? Who’s the impostor? Outsmart your foes or get eliminated.

"Impostor Coil"

One-third of players are secretly AI-controlled bots.

Real players must guess who’s fake and vote them out.

Guess wrong, and you get eliminated instead.

"Whispers in the Dark"

In pitch black, each player hears different audio clues.

Must decide who is lying about what they heard to sabotage the others.

"Lie Detector"

Players answer lore or trivia questions.

Others guess if they’re lying.

Too many wrong guesses or lies = elimination.
