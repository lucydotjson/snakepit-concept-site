SNAKEPIT

Multiplayer Survival Party Game

"Survive the jungle. Outsmart the bots. Don’t trust anyone."

Genre: Survival / Party Elimination
Players: 30 per match
Platform: PC + console (maybe)
Rating: Teen (13+)

You and 29 others are kidnapped by a mysterious person who calls himself "the king Cobra" and dropped in a jungle dome. Compete in 6 twisted Trials, then battle it out in the deadly final round: The Snakepit.

Trial Categories

Mind – Puzzles, memory Strength – Timing, force Agility – Movement, reflexes Deception – Bluff, detect lies Social – Alliances & betrayal Chaos – Total unpredictability
