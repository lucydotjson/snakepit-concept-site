Mind Trials — Survive or Lose Everything

The Code of Silence

Team of 3 players

You wake up locked in separate cells. Each holds part of a code.

Use only light signals to share your clues and unlock the door.

One mistake and you’ll feel the shock. Fail, and it’s over.

Circuit of Death

Solo player

You enter a room filled with switches connected to deadly circuits.

Activate them in the right order to escape.

A wrong move resets progress — and brings pain.

Reflections of Terror

Pair of players

One sees the path ahead, the other can’t see a thing.

The guide must talk the blind player through the maze.

One wrong step ends it all.
